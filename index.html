Privacy Policy

We Value Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how KinetIQ ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our apps and services. This policy complies with Apple's App Store requirements, including transparency on data handling as per Apple's privacy nutrition labels.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data except for Motion Sensor Data, which is necessary for the core functionality of our apps. This includes data from device sensors such as accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers to track motion and orientation. All processing occurs on-device, and no data is transmitted off-device without explicit user consent.

  • Motion Sensor Data: Real-time readings from device motion sensors, used solely for app features like activity tracking or gesture recognition.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers, usage data, device information, location data, or any sensitive information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use Motion Sensor Data exclusively to:

  • Provide core app functionality, such as real-time motion analysis and user interactions.
  • Improve app performance through on-device processing (no data is sent to servers).
  • Comply with legal obligations if required.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data. Motion Sensor Data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Security and Retention

We use industry-standard security measures to protect data on your device. Motion Sensor Data is retained only during active app use and is deleted upon app closure or user request.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Disable sensor access in device settings to stop collection.
  • Request deletion of any temporary data by contacting us at privacy@kinetiq.com.
  • For California residents: Rights under CCPA/CPRA (though minimal data is collected).
  • For EU/UK residents: Rights under GDPR, including access and erasure (applicable to any stored data).

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with the effective date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at privacy@kinetiq.com.
